Sauteed Potatoes

Little cubes of seasoned crispy fried potatoes with a fluffy interior. These saut√©ed potatoes take around 30 minutes, and they're a fantastic accompaniment to that special dinner.

How to make these Sauteed Potatoes

Cut and cube the potatoes

Heat frying pan to a medium high heat

Place cubed potatoes in a large frying pan hot oil with a lid, turning often until very lightly browned.

Then leave cover on and set heat to a simmer, Then leave for 10 to 12 minutes

Sprinkle on salt and pepper.

Turn off the heat, allow to cool then your ready to serve.
